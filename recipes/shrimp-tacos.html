Shrimp Tacos

Description

Are you ready for the best shrimp tacos ever? You know Im a fan of shrimp recipes, but Ill be honest, these shrimp tacos might just be my favorite. They satisfy my cravings for spicy Mexican flavors, are approachable enough for an easy weeknight dinner, and will hit the spot as a crowd pleaser. Id say thats an unbeatable combo!

Ingredients

Spicy Shrimp

  • Shrimp
  • Seasoning: chipotle powder, cumin powder, salt, and pepper
  • Olive Oil

Cilantro Lime Bacon Slaw

  • Bacon
  • Green Cabbage
  • Cilantro Leaves
  • Olive Oil & Lime Juice

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon.
  2. Make the slaw.
  3. Prep the shrimp.
  4. Assemble ingredients.
